KittyWars for iOS
February 2016
MHacks 2016

Three of my friends and I travelled to MHacks at the University of Michigan to build a project together. After spending 8 hours trying to figure out what to make, we decided that we should dedicate our first hackathon to "having fun," so naturally that means making a game about cats.

In the game, the player chooses to join one of two teams: ninja kitties or pirate kitties. They are then assigned certain attacks based on their team, and can level up as they beat opponents.
