Interactive Technology Intern at Droga5
June 2016 - August 2016
New York, NY

At Droga5, I got to see the agency process for seeing a project all the way through -from UX to creative design, development, and QA.

I created an internal, client facing database for the Technology team’s banner production. I built and managed a relational database with PHP and mySQL, and used HTML, SASS, and JavaScript (Grunt, jQuery and GSAP) for the front-end development.

I also got the chance to design a page of the site -- an inspiration page that included examples of interesting, award-winning ads that showcased the types, sizes, and styles of banner ads. Design- wise, I wanted the page to focus on suiting the needs of the creatives, so there was a strong emphasis on visualization of the advertisements, along with an informative hover state, and a modal that brought the user to a video clip of interaction with the ad.

With this project and other internal projects, we followed SCRUM workflow, doing development and QA in pieces, all organized with JIRA.

Droga5’s Intern-wide project put us into teams across all departments, where we researched and shared upcoming trends in the world. My group focused on chatbots in messenger technologies. Brands like Sephora and CNN are putting rudimentary chatbots on messenger platforms, and I spoke a lot about the limitations of such bots. I also played with Wit.ai, an API for chatbot development. Reach out to me if you want to know more about what I learned!
